Master the Run: How to Play

Basic Controls

  • SPACE - Jump: Leap over trains and obstacles to stay in the run.
  • ← → - Change Lanes: Switch lanes to collect coins and avoid upcoming dangers.
  • ↓ - Slide: Duck under high obstacles and trains approaching at ground level.

Game Objectives

  • Collect Coins: Gather coins scattered along the tracks to charge up powerful power-ups.
  • Avoid Obstacles and Trains: Dodge various obstacles and oncoming trains to prolong your run and increase your score.
  • Beat Your High Score: Push your limits and strive to achieve the highest score possible with each run.
  • Unlock New Characters: Use collected coins or achievements to unlock a diverse roster of unique characters.

Pro Tips for Subway Supremacy

  • Master Combos: Combine jumps and slides fluidly to navigate complex obstacle patterns and maximize coin collection.
  • Pattern Recognition: Pay close attention to track patterns to anticipate obstacles and train movements in advance.
  • Gear Upgrades: Invest coins to upgrade your power-up durations and effectiveness for longer and more rewarding runs.
  • Power-Up Strategy: Deploy power-ups strategically to overcome challenging sections and significantly boost your score.
